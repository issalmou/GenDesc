## 1. Introduction

**DescriptoAI** is a SaaS tool that uses a fine-tuned AI vision-language model (BLIP) to automatically generate premium, marketing-ready fashion product descriptions from images.

Whether you run a Shopify boutique, manage a large clothing catalogue, or are a content manager for a fashion brand, DescriptoAI eliminates hours of manual copywriting — letting you focus on what matters: selling great fashion.

### What makes DescriptoAI different?

- The model is **specifically fine-tuned on fashion e-commerce data**, not a generic AI.
- Descriptions focus on **garment-specific attributes**: silhouette, fabric, colour, fit, and style.
- The output is **immediately publishable** — professional, conversion-optimised copy.
- Supports both **file upload** and **image URL** as input methods.

## 3. How to Use DescriptoAI

### 3.1 — Upload an Image

1. Scroll to the **"Generate a Fashion Description Instantly"** section (or click **Try It Free** in the hero).
2. Make sure the **Upload Image** tab is selected (it is by default).
3. **Drag and drop** your clothing or accessory photo onto the dashed upload zone, **or click the zone** to open your file browser.
4. Supported formats: **JPG, JPEG, PNG, WebP** — maximum file size **10 MB**.
5. A preview of your image will appear in the upload zone.
6. Click **Generate Description**.
7. Wait a few seconds while the AI analyses your fashion item.
8. The generated description appears on the right side of the card.
9. Click **Copy** to copy it to your clipboard.

---

### 3.2 — Use an Image URL

1. Click the **Image URL** tab at the top of the demo card.
2. Paste a direct image link into the input field (e.g. `https://example.com/dress.jpg`).
   - The URL must point directly to an image file (ending in `.jpg`, `.jpeg`, `.png`, `.webp`, etc.).
   - The image must be **publicly accessible** (not behind a login or paywall).
3. Click **Preview** to load and verify the image.
4. If the image loads correctly, click **Generate Description**.
5. The AI description will appear on the right.
6. Click **Copy** to copy it to your clipboard.

> **Note:** If the image fails to load, check that the URL is correct and that the image is not restricted. Try downloading the image and using the file upload method instead.

---

### 3.3 — Generate a Description

After selecting your input (upload or URL):

1. Click the **Generate Description** button.
2. The button will change to **"Analysing outfit…"** and a spinner will appear while the AI processes your image.
3. Processing typically takes **3–8 seconds** (may be longer if the server is warming up).
4. The description will appear in the dark result panel on the right.
5. Click **Copy** to copy the text to your clipboard — the button will briefly show **"Copied!"** to confirm.
6. Paste the description directly into your product listing on Shopify, WooCommerce, or any other platform.

---

### 3.4 — Reset and Start Again

Click the **Reset** button at any time to:
- Clear the uploaded image or URL
- Remove the generated description
- Start fresh with a new fashion item

## 5. Tips for Best Results

### Image Quality
- Use **clear, well-lit photos** with a neutral or white background when possible.
- Make sure the **garment is the main focus** of the image — avoid cluttered backgrounds.
- **Front-facing product shots** typically yield the most detailed descriptions.
- Avoid heavily edited or illustrated images; realistic photos work best.

### Image Content
- The AI performs best with **single garments or complete outfits**.
- Accessories (bags, shoes, jewellery) are supported but may yield shorter descriptions.
- Include **contextual details** like a model wearing the item if you want style and occasion references in the output.

### File Size and Format
- Keep images under **10 MB** for reliable upload.
- **JPEG** files at high quality (75–90%) are the best balance of quality and speed.
- Avoid heavily compressed or pixelated images.

### Using Image URLs
- Always use a **direct image URL** (the URL should end with an image extension).
- Avoid URLs from platforms that block hotlinking (e.g. some Instagram or Pinterest links).
- Product image URLs from Shopify, WooCommerce, or CDN-hosted stores work well.

### After Generation
- The description is a strong starting point — feel free to **personalise it** with your brand's tone of voice, specific size info, or care instructions.
- For consistent results across a catalogue, keep the photo style uniform (same lighting, background, and angle).

## 6. FAQ

**Q: How long does generation take?**  
A: Typically 3–8 seconds. If the server is cold-starting after a period of inactivity, the first request may take up to 30 seconds. Simply try again if you see a timeout error.

**Q: Why is the URL Preview button not working?**  
A: The URL must point directly to a publicly hosted image file. Some websites block image hotlinking. Try saving the image locally and using the Upload tab instead.

**Q: Can I use screenshots or catalogue PDFs?**  
A: Screenshots of clothing items work, but a clean product photo will yield better results. PDFs are not supported — extract the image first.

**Q: The description doesn't match my product — what happened?**  
A: This can occur with very unusual angles, heavy stylisation, or low-resolution images. Try a clearer, front-facing photo of the garment.

**Q: Are there any file types that don't work?**  
A: The accepted formats are JPG, JPEG, PNG, and WebP. GIF, TIFF, BMP, HEIC, and SVG files are not supported.

**Q: Is there a limit on how many descriptions I can generate?**  
A: In the current demo, there is no enforced hard limit. Heavy usage may result in temporary slowdowns.

**Q: Is my data safe?**  
A: Yes. Images are sent securely to the API, processed to generate the description, and immediately discarded. Nothing is stored on any server.

**Q: Can I use the generated descriptions commercially?**  
A: Yes — the descriptions generated are yours to use freely on your products, listings, and marketing materials.
